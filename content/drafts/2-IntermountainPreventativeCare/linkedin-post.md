# LinkedIn Post Draft

New blog post is live.

This one looks at avoidable ED visits and what data can actually tell us about preventative care. I built a geographic risk dashboard using public data from the CDC, Census, and @Intermountain Health facility locations to identify which communities in Utah are at the highest risk. No patient records, no clinical data. Social determinants of health alone explained nearly all the variation in avoidable ED risk (R² = 0.97).

This project builds on BYU Healthcare Case Competitions with my teammates Harrison Nemelka, Gracie King, Colby Clark, and our coach Heather Thiesset. Full post with sources and dashboard on samwich-analytics.com.

For anyone working in healthcare or public health, what has your experience been with data-driven preventative care?
